The Three Breakthroughs

Why This Actually Works

"Not 'agents with different prompts.' Purpose-built intelligence."

1. Task DAG

Not a to-do list. A directed acyclic graph with dependencies, parallelism, and dynamic re-planning. If a co-pilot discovers something unexpected, the system re-plans in real-time.

2. Three-Layer Memory

📌 Episodic → What's happening NOW
🧠 Semantic → What we KNOW
🔄 Procedural → What we've LEARNED

After a month, Parallax knows your preferences cold.

3. Earned Trust

Co-pilots start cautious and earn autonomy — like a new team member becoming a trusted colleague. Thresholds calibrate per-user, per-task-type, over time.
